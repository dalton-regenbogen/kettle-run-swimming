Home of Kettle Run Swimming

Welcome to KR Swimming—established in 2008. We’re a program built on consistent effort, team-first support, and pride in representing our school the right way. We welcome both year-round and seasonal athletes who are committed to improving, encouraging their teammates, and bringing positive energy to the deck every day. No matter your experience level, if you’re ready to work, learn, and contribute to the team, there’s a place for you here at KR Swimming.
